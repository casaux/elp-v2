What is the assessee's requirement for the English Language Proficiency notification?

If the assessee's English Language Proficiency notification is required as part of an Air Traffic Controller licence, please complete the English Language Proficiency form on the CASA website.

Date of assessment

For example, 31 01 2020

The person is able to demonstrate her or his ability to do the following:









Select at least one of following documents indicated below, that you have sighted as evidence that the assessee meets the general English Language Proficiency standards:






Level 4 Level 5 Level 6 Assessment category
Pronunciation, ease of understanding
Grammatical structure and sentence patterns
Appropriate vocabulary and phraseologies
Fluency, natural flow of speech, varied style for emphasis, appropriate marker usage
Comprehension accuracy and including comprehension of linguistic and cultural subtleties
Ease of interaction and appropriate response to verbal and non-verbal cues

The assessee will be issued with the ELP assessment level corresponding to the lowest of these scores.

  • If the lowest score is 4, they will be awarded an ELP Level 4, valid for a period of 3 years only
  • If the lowest score is 5, they will be awarded an ELP Level 5, valid for a period of 6 years only
  • If the lowest score is 6, they will be awarded an ELP Level 6, valid permanently, i.e. it does not need to be renewed

Level to be issued: Level 4
